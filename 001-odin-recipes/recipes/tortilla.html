Tortilla de Patatas

Spanish (potato) omelette

Description

The traditional Spanish tortilla de patatas: a potato omelette that leaves nobody indifferent

Ingredients

  • 4-5 Potatoes
  • 1-2 Onions
  • 4-6 Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Heat a large amount of oil in a frying pan
  2. Mince the onion and to place in the oil
  3. Cut the potato into small pieces and place in the oil
  4. Remove everythin from the pan, and mix the eggs with the fried onion and potato
  5. Heat the pan and make the omelet
